My Major Releases

Spaceflight EP

This is my latest work at the moment, and one I'm very proud of.
This is a track I had in mind since a bit, and decided it couldn't stay in one genre only. I then tagged my friend Dion Zijlmans along for an awesome remix!

Novus

A track that opens up a portal to another universe. Remixes by Garmeid and Bread Snakes finish to complete the picture.

Entropy

One of my older Trance compositions, I did it for a producer contest using only free plugins available to the public. It has become one of my favorite track ever made, because it is unique, and is the closest to Tech Trance I got.

Lorentz Violation

Originally from Dion Zijlmans, I couldn't resist but use it to unleash some of my power Trance creativity!

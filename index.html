My Major Releases

Spaceflight EP

This is my latest work at the moment, and one I'm very proud of. This continuous EP explores space travel in a journey from our planet to the rings of Saturn.

Novus

I brought my friends Michael Fuller and Dion Zijlmans on this one, and composed "Novus", an emotional Trance track about us.

Entropy

One of my older Trance compositions, I did it for a producer contest using only free plugins available to the public. It has become one of my favorite track ever made, because it is unique, and is the closest to Tech Trance I got.

Lorentz Violation

Made with power (and love), Lorentz Violation decpits FTL travel in a 140bpm track across the Milky Way towards other galaxies.
